What Exactly is Perfume Blending?

Perfume blending is the cornerstone of fragrance creation.

It entails the precise fusing of diverse natural and synthetic aroma materials that will render a particular olfactory portrait that seamlessly balances the various notes that comprise the formulation. It delicately blends essential oils and other chemicals in precise and masterful proportions, utilizing cutting edge skills derived from both the realms of art and science.

It is a process of trial and error squared that is so much more than just an additive exercise, and requires great patience as well as consummate mastery. Blending diverse compounds form exciting new interactions that lead to never-before-seen olfactory profiles. Temperature, pH measures of acidity and choice of solvent can affect molecular bonding, influencing the final formulation. Mixing two similar but diverse elements can render a scent with a more distinct character than either would alone.
